About

Researcher and educator focused on blockchain technology, cybersecurity and distributed applications. I develop decentralized applications, build and operate Hyperledger Besu (QBFT) networks and turn those systems into clear, hands‑on learning material.

I teach at the University of West Attica (UniWA) and the University of Malta. As a Senior Researcher I contribute to EU and Greek National projects. I am co‑author of the first open‑access Greek book on blockchain (Kallipos editions).

  • PhD in Computer Networks— with focus on distributed systems & security
  • Build and run a Besu QBFT network with Blockscout & Faucet for teaching/research
  • Courses & workshops on: Blockchain, P2P Networks, CyberSecurity
  • Interests: zero knowledge proofs (ZKPs), oracles, protocol hardening, validator ops, verifiable credentials
  • Member of INATBA's Academic Advisory Board
  • Associate Editor on “Blockchain for Web3 and the Metaverse” at Frontiers in Blockchain
