About Dimitris

Greetings! I am Dimitris G. Kogias, a dedicated scholar and instructor specializing in blockchain technology, Web3 innovations, and cybersecurity. My academic journey is enriched by my roles at the University of West Attica and the University of Malta, where I engage in groundbreaking research and inspire future leaders in these dynamic fields.

Driven by a deep interest in distributed systems, my work explores the transformative potential of decentralized applications and secure digital frameworks. I am passionate about cultivating a collaborative environment that promotes learning, innovation, and the advancement of technology.

Highlighted Research Initiatives

  • Decentralized Identity Systems: Crafting blockchain-based solutions that ensure privacy and security in digital identities.
  • Smart Contract Vulnerability Analysis: Investigating security flaws in Ethereum smart contracts and developing countermeasures.
  • Performance Optimization for DApps: Creating scalable and efficient architectures for decentralized applications.

Development Portfolio

Explore a curated selection of projects that reflect my technical skills and research interests:

Courses & Teaching Philosophy

I offer a range of courses designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in blockchain and cybersecurity:

  • Blockchain Fundamentals: A foundational course covering the principles and applications of blockchain technology.
  • Web3 Development: Hands-on training in building decentralized applications using contemporary Web3 frameworks and tools.
  • Cybersecurity Best Practices: Instruction on protecting digital assets and infrastructures through effective security strategies.

My instructional approach integrates theoretical concepts with experiential learning to foster deep understanding and real-world application.
