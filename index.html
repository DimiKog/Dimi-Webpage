About

I am a researcher and lecturer working at the intersection of blockchain security, distributed consensus, and Web3 education. Recent work includes a Hyperledger Besu QBFT network (5 validators), a gamified learning app Proof of Escape (PoE), and an LDL Fantasy DApp with SBT profiles and trade governance.

  • PhD in Computer Networks
  • Private Besu PoA/QBFT network + Blockscout + faucet
  • Teaching: Blockchain, P2P Networks, Cybersecurity
